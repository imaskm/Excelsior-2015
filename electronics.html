Robo-Olympic

★a) Pull the weight using your robot
  • Weights: 1/2, 1, 2 Kg
  • Team Specification:
    A team may consists of maximum 4 members and minimum 2 members. A team can use any bot. Inter college teams can be formed. About the Bot: Bot can be wired or wireless. Bot should be capable of changing frequency to avoid interference in case of wireless bots. Any type of robot can participate.
  • Objective:
    The participant's bot has to reach the finish line pulling weight in the minimum time. Pull the weight until weight crosses the finish line.
    Judging Criteria:
  • 1) Maximum distance covered.
  • 2) Maximum weight pulled by the bot.
    ★b) Control Test of Bot :
  • Description: The track can be elevated, steep down and turns.
  • Track Details: Track will be 9 inch wide. Track will be upto 10 meters.
  • Team Specification: A team may consists of maximum 4 members and minimum 2 members. A team can use any bot. Inter college teams can be formed.
  • Rules: One start at a time. Maximum three retry. Retry will be initiated from the last position of the bot on the track.
  • Time limit: 5 minutes will be provided to complete the track.
  • Points award: 10 points will be given for every 1 metre crossing. 5 points will be deducted for every retry.
  • Judging Criteria: 1) Maximum distance covered.
    ★c) Free style bot race:
  • Description: the competition starts with bot race from initial poinrt to reach the final point by covering the track which will be upto 50 meters.
  • Track details: track may have jumps, speed breakers, pits
  • Team Specification: A team may consists of maximum 4 members and minimum 2 members. A team can use any bot. Inter college teams can be formed.
  • About the Bot: Bot can be wired or wireless. Bot should be capable of changing frequency to avoid interference in case of wireless bots. Any type of robot can participate.
  • Rules: Maximum three retry. Retry will be initiated from the last position of the bot on the track.
  • Points award: 10 points will be given for every 1 metre crossing. 2 points will be deducted for every retry. For every perfect jump with safe landing 20 points will be given.
  • Judging Criteria:
  • 1) Maximum Distance covered.
  • 2) Minimum Time taken. if all the bots fails to complete the race.
    ★d) Battle:
  • Description: All the bots will be in the ring and they will try to push their opponents out of the ring and also there will be surprises in the ring .
  • Objective: Knockout your opponent out of the ring or to trash it out.
  • Rules: If any bot can't overcome form surprise it will be eliminated.
  • Judging Criteria: Winners will be decide from last three bots remaining in the ring.
