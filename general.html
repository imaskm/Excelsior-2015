    ★Nail Art

    ☆Head Coordinator – Jyoti Garg


    This is a team event . Each team will have four members. This event is further divided into four levels where the total score of every team from individual event will be compiled and a winner will be declared.


    ☆Levels :

    Level one:

  • Two members from each team will participate in the THREE LEG RACE. The last three teams will be eliminated.
  • Requirements: Rope/cloth.

    Level Two :

    Under this level the game of drop the bottle will be conducted and one member from each team will participate in it. The last three teams will be eliminated.
  • Requirements: Solid bottles, balls, napkins, bags.

    Level Three

    in this level all the team members will participate in the activity and each one will have to hold one leg of the other member except one and will have to pick up the ball from the basket and dropping it at the other side while still holding each others legs. The one team droping maximum no. of balls will win the game.The last two teams will be eliminated.
  • Requirements: Baskets, balls/balloons.

    Level Four :

    Two members will be called from each team. Both the participants will be facing in the opposite directions and resting on each others back. One will pick up a chit which may contain a name of a personality, a popular face etc. The other team members will have to make a guess to what is in the slip , they can ask only a max of ten questions to their team mate who can only give a yes / no type of answer to their question. This will be repeated two or three times with each team and the one able to make the maximum number of guess wins.
