“Abhinaya”




X

Abhinaya

Rules

  •     The skit can be in any recognized Indian language.
  •     Total participants will be 6-10.
  •     Sufficient thought and care should go into the choice of the skit keeping in view that it suits the occasion and serves the taste of general student viewer/audience.
  •     The depiction of scenes align or repugnant to our traditions, culture, social life etc. should be avoided.
  •     The theme, dialogues and presentation of the skit should not be vulgar or objectionable.
  •     In case of technical failure , the teams are allowed to resume from last scene.
  •     The duration of the performance will be 8-10 minutes.
